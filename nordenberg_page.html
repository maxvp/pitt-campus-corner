John J.
Awesome dorm space with spacious rooms and bathrooms. I’ve a few complaints, though: -I think they only clean bathrooms once over the weekends, which is when the bathrooms get the most traffic so it quickly gets messy. -The WIFI isn’t the best, even when connected to Ethernet (understandable, it’s a busy place) Otherwise, it’s a great place to live and you should consider yourself lucky if you get in to this FRESHMAN-ONLY dormitory. Double, triple, and quadruple person dorms. 2 Study rooms on each floor. I feel safe in this dorm. Hail to Pitt!
Joe Panini
MUST READ. Best freshman residence hall, period. Access to pretty much everything including fitness, health offices and markets within a 10 minute walk. Sits in the center of campus. Competitive dorm to get into first year as it is very new, is air conditioned, and have quality bathrooms. If you get in, you’re part of the elite. Superb dorm for an excellent university.
