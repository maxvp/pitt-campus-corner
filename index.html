Pitt Campus Corner

Your destination for all Pitt related housing information.

Sutherland Hall

Sutherland Hall is an upper-campus first-year residence hall with doubles and suites.

Litchfield Towers

The Litchfield Towers A-C are primarily first-year residence halls with singles and doubles located in the heart of campus.

Nordenberg Hall

Nordenberg Hall is the newest first-year residence hall featuring doubles and triples.

Bouquet Gardens

Bouquet Gardens provides on-campus apartment style housing for upperclassmen.

Ruskin Hall

Ruskin Hall is an upperclassmen residence hall that provides apartment-style housing.

Lothrop Hall

Lothrop Hall is a first-year and upperclassmen residence hall featuring singles and doubles.
